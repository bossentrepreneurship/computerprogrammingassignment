Work Outside Of Work Hours

Even though most employers hire you to work from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, they expect you to work outside of the work hours they assigned you. They want you to work extra so that you meet the deadlines for projects. This usually means that you have to work for 1-2 hours after work is over and 2-3 hours on weekends. In total, you might have to work from 50-60 hours weekly.

Job Perks

One of the better thing of being a software engineer is that you work from an office or from your home. This means that you can still work for a company if you live in a another country. Another perk is that you get 2-3 weeks of vacation a year. Your work hours are also flexible. If you finish all you work, you can stop working, even if it is during your work hours (you still have to attend meetings). Finally, depending on your experience and current salary, you can get bonuses and promotions based on your performance. These bonuses can earn you up to $5000 annually.
