Job Perks

One of the better thing of being a software engineer is that you can work from an office or from your home. This means that you can still work for a company if you live in a another country. Another perk is that you get 3-5 weeks of vacation a year. Your work hours are also flexible. If you finish all your work, you can stop working, even if it is during your work hours (you still have to attend meetings). Finally, depending on your experience and current salary, you can get bonuses and promotions based on your performance. These bonuses can earn you up to $25, 000 annually.
