University

To be a software engineer, you will need university education. You have to complete university with a bachelor's degree in computer science or software engineering. This means that you will need to stay at university for at least 4 years.

Other Reccomendations

Employers will not only look at the things you do in school. They will also look at what you do outside of school. Your odds of being hired will drastically improve if you get involved in extracurricular activities. These activities may include participating in hackathonsAn event in which a large number of people meet up to engage in collaborative computer programming to solve problems or build programs., coding competitionsAn event where people try to be the first to solve coding problems., and even going to coding bootcampA camp where people learn to write and apply code. These camps can last anywhere from a week to half a year. To apply to coding bootcamp, you do not need any prior experience.. Although it is not required, being able to communicate in a different language will not hurt your chances of being hired.
